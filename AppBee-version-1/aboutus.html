Who We Are ?

                        Appbee is an application design & development firm founded in Jan-2015 by Shreevatsa Kanetkar and Shankar Divekar. At appbee we are passionate and enthusiastic team of developers who are keen on crafting & delivering innovative solutions. Our agile nature of working makes way for us to instill and align to newer technologies. At the core we believe in delivering the most competent & quality product for which we have collaborated with design & development companies across the globe. Our strengths are usability engineering, information architecture, user interface (UI), web & mobile application design & development across platforms and deploying the projects on higly scalable cloud hosting.In April-2018 Deepak Doddur has joined Appbee as partner keeping the long term growth prospects and unlimited future opportunities.
